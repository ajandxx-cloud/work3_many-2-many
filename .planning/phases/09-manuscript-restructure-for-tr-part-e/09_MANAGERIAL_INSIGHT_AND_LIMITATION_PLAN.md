Convert the old policy-oriented manuscript material into simulation-based
managerial insights bounded by Phase 8. This artifact is not final manuscript
prose and does not authorize direct city policy recommendations.

## Recommended Managerial Section Structure

1. Begin with evidence boundaries: synthetic-only evidence, lower served share,
   uncalibrated passenger preferences, and no real/semi-real validation.
2. Present a compact table of condition, observation, managerial interpretation,
   and limitation.
3. Tie each insight to a specific evidence family.
4. Move unsupported policy maps, deployment tiers, gamma frontier, and weight
   sensitivity claims to appendix or remove them.
5. End by stating what a real deployment study would need to calibrate.

## Required Limitations

1. Synthetic-only evidence.
2. No real or semi-real Beijing validation.
3. No empirically calibrated passenger preferences.
4. Passenger types are simulation-range constructs.
5. Matched-coverage controls have 15 durable failed FullModel rows.
6. Fixed accepted-set results are diagnostic and do not represent natural
   behavioral served-share outcomes.
7. Robustness grids are diagnostic and reduced, not full global sensitivity or
   universal parameter evidence.
8. Paired hypothesis tests are not implemented; current uncertainty evidence is
   paired bootstrap confidence intervals where available.
9. ALNS remains heuristic evidence, not an exact global optimality proof.
