# Plan 09-01: TR-E Manuscript Architecture and Claim-Gate Map

<objective>
Create the source-of-truth Phase 9 manuscript architecture artifact that converts
the current manuscript from a TR-A-style policy/method draft into a TR-E
evidence-chain structure, while explicitly blocking final claim wording on the
missing Phase 8 claim-gate artifacts.
</objective>

<must_haves>
- D-01: Main storyline is evidence-chain reconstruction, not unconditional method superiority.
- D-05: Use a TR-E evidence-chain structure rather than preserving the current section skeleton unchanged.
- D-06: Combine model and algorithm material into `Framework and Solution Approach`.
- D-07: Organize experiments as design first, then evidence packages.
- D-08: Place limitations before managerial insights.
- D-09: Use the approved high-level outline from Phase 9 context.
- D-15: Rename policy section concept to `Managerial Insights and Boundary Conditions`.
- D-17: Beijing/city language must stay `Beijing-inspired synthetic scenario` unless later evidence supports more.
</must_haves>

<truths>
- `manuscript/main.tex` still names Transportation Research Part A and currently includes sections in the old order.
- Phase 8 claim-gate artifacts are absent from this workspace and must be treated as blocking prerequisites for final claims.
- TR-E is logistics-centered and accepts simulation/operations research methods when the evidence chain and data transparency are clear.
- Phase 2 and Phase 6 contracts control comparison-family, metric-denominator, and formal-evidence language.
</truths>
